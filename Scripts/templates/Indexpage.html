DBL HTI + Webtech

This website is made by students of the Eindhoven University of Technology as a part of the course DBL HTI + Webtech.
This website is designed to visualize large datasets in CSV formats, in order to help people process and learn from this data.

The team consists of seven students:
Eva Boots, Mathew Chen, Nick Jeurissen, Naomi Han, Sven Mokveld, Athanasios Papamichail and Ole Wouters.
The students within this group study three different bachelor programs, creating an optimal combination of knowledge.
We hope that this website helps you with your visualization needs!

Data visualisation

On our website there are multiple different types of data visualisation, which can be found by clicking on the button above.
This will direct you to the dual visualisation, which will display your data in both a matrix and a node link diagram.
On this page you'll the directions to the other webpages, if you would wish to see one of the visualisations in more detail.
